# Monetization Playbook - tool-call-finetune-lab

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B model adaptation and eval
- **Primary buyer:** AI platform teams, applied ML engineers, and model evaluation groups.
- **Revenue wedge:** QLoRA/BFCL/tool-call adaptation lab with serving-readiness notes.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Model adaptation study** - pricing hypothesis: $5k-$15k eval study
2. **BFCL-style evaluation pack** - pricing hypothesis: $15k-$60k adaptation sprint
3. **Serving readiness review** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$12k/month model eval ops

## Acquisition Channels

- Model evaluation content
- StagePilot comparison
- Applied ML team outreach

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- make verify passes
- Training config is explicit
- GPU paths are separated

## Risk Boundaries

- Dataset licensing required
- Eval coverage before deployment
- No model-quality guarantees without runs

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish eval template
- **60 days:** Create dataset license checklist
- **90 days:** Offer adaptation assessment

## Metrics That Matter

- Tool-call accuracy
- Eval coverage
- Serving latency posture

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
